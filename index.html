About Us

FTC Files is a community-based library containing various parts of the FIRST Engineering Notebook, such as: outreach entries, business plans, daily logs, team summaries, and much more. This network of archives is built on sharing, so teams across the board can publish their notebooks/tips for the upcoming seasons. This will serve as a resource to help rookie teams or veteran teams get a better sense of the quantity and quality of most notebooks, and can even browse some award-winning information. The notebooks will be filtered through the moderators and admins of the discord server, and there will be a featured page for notebooks that meet outstanding criteria, or those that have won awards at state/national championships. Another cool thing that we plan to do is get documentation from actual engineers from places such as NASA, SpaceX, Google, Apple, and many other organizations. This can help motivate teams by showing them the real application of the engineering notebook, especially rookies.
